What Is Gunpowder?

Gunpowder is the earliest known chemical explosive.
It was discovered by Chinese chemists who were on the hunt for immortality. Gunpowder is not only used in wartime, but also in celebration. Gunpowder is used in making fireworks. Once a firework is in the sky, gunpowder is what causes it to BOOM and SPARKLE! It consists of a mixture of sulfur, charcoal, and potassium nitrate. The sulfur and charcoal act as fuels while the saltpeter is an oxidizer

How Gunpowder Has Impacted Our Lives Today

Gunpowder is the basis of many modern weapons. Guns, grenades...We use it to fight. It helps us win battles! Yet behind all this destruction, we can see fireworks. we feel happy, excited, or scared. Gunpowder is so powerful that it's really hard to believe that this was originally made to be an elixir of life.
