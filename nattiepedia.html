The Name

The name "Nattiepedia" isn't just random, it has got some logic behind it. "Nattie" comes from my name, Natalie. it's my preferred nickname and can be also spelt as "Natty", "Natee" and "Nattee". However, I chose "Nattie" because it definitely looks the cutest in text! Apart from that, my second favourite spelling of "nattie" is "natty" which is actually already a word. The word "natty" is an adjective that comes from the 18th century. Originally it was a slang term, perhaps related to the adjective neat. These days, if you say someone “looks neat,” it might sound like they look clean, but it also can mean that they look fashionable and put together. Sweet but irrelevant. It may give people the wrong idea on what Nattiepedia truly is. So,I also chose "nattie" to avoid confusion. Now you might be also wondering where I got "pedia" from. If you haven't already guessed it yet, Nattiepedia is almost a mini version of Wikipedia. "Pedia" means "Related to learning", and it is true that nattiepedia is related to learning with all it's fun facts. And the name "Nattiepedia" definitely has a nice ring to it, huh?
